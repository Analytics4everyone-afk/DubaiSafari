Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for the desert safari?
Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothes. We recommend light cotton clothing, closed-toe shoes or sandals, and sunglasses. Avoid high heels or formal wear. In winter months (Nov-Feb), bring a light jacket as desert evenings can be cool.
Is the desert safari safe for children and elderly?
Yes! Our safari is suitable for all ages. Children under 3 and pregnant women should skip the dune bashing but can enjoy all other activities. For elderly guests, we can arrange a gentler dune bashing experience upon request.
Do you provide hotel pickup and drop-off?
Yes, all our evening safari packages include complimentary pickup and drop-off from hotels in Dubai, Sharjah, and Ajman. For quad bike and buggy rides, pickup is available for an additional fee. We'll confirm pickup time 24 hours before your tour.
What is included in the BBQ buffet dinner?
Our BBQ buffet includes grilled meats (chicken, lamb, beef kebabs), Arabic bread, hummus, salads, rice dishes, fresh fruits, and desserts. Vegetarian options are available. Unlimited soft drinks, water, tea, and coffee are included.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the tour. Cancellations within 24 hours are non-refundable. In case of bad weather, we'll reschedule your tour at no extra cost or provide a full refund.
